Seals vs. Sea Lions

Seals and Sea Lions are both marine mammals, but they have key differences:

Differences Between Seals and Sea Lions

  • Seals have small ear holes, while sea lions have external ear flaps.
  • Seals use their hind flippers for swimming, whereas sea lions use their front flippers.
  • Sea lions can walk on land using their large flippers, while seals move by wriggling on their bellies.

Habitat and Behavior

  1. Seals are more solitary, while sea lions are social and live in large groups.
  2. Sea lions are typically noisier, known for their loud barks, whereas seals are quieter.
  3. Seals prefer icy or cold waters, while sea lions thrive in warmer coastal areas.
